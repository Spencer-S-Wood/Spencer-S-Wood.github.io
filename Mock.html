Week 10-11: Unsupervised Learning – Clustering.

The hypothesis tests whether UK cities will be clustered into the 12 UK regions based on their longitudinal and latitudinal bearing.
Most regions are clustered fairly accurately, apart from London and the South East. Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland are also less clear. The colab can be found here.
