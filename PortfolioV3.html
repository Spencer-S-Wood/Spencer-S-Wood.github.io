Week 2: Data can be deceptive.....

The first chart suggests IMF lending was greatest in 2022. However the IMF actually lent the most (as a % of World GDP) in 1984. This suggests 1984 was actually the most risky year to invest, not 2022. This is because in 1984 the outstanding credit to GDP ratio is greater.
